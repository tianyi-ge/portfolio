About me

I'm a part-time research assistant at IPADS at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and a master's student at University of Michigan.

My primary research focuses on distributed systems and operating systems. I'm interested in topics e.g. distributed storage, consensus algorithm, cloud computing, etc.

At UMich I worked on distributed storage and databases with Prof. Harsha V. Madhyastha, Muhammed Uluyol, and Tianyu (Kevin) Chen. Also, I worked with Prof. Chinedum Okwudire on a cloud 3D printing platform Ulendo based on AWS.

During my undergrad I worked with Prof. Xudong Wang (SJTU) and Dr. Jiawei Zhang (SJTU) on task containerization and scheduling in edge computing. Besides, I was an intern for 3 months at Huawei Intelligent EdgeFabric.
